Video review and photos of the complex
ADVANTAGES OF THE LOCATION:
A house on the Kura embankment, along which you can walk to any place of interest in the center
Convenient interchange, this is the middle of the city and the entrance and exit to two central highways, Tsereteli Avenue and Baratashvili Embankment, next to two bridges connecting the left and right sides of Tbilisi, it is easy to get to the airport and anywhere in the city, it will be easy to get to the new airport
Super views from the windows, in front of the house is the Kura embankment and the right mountain bank of the river
Near the center of clinics, 10 minutes by car
Nearby are the Prosecutor General's Office of Tbilisi and the court, many civil servants and people who have come on vacation want to rent an apartment in this house
The central branch of the Bank of Georgia is across the river, it employs 8000 people, and the main branch of TBS is being built next to it on 200 thousand square meters. Their employees also need apartments
There is no supermarket in this location, it will appear in our house - Agrohub
A three-minute walk away is the largest exhibition center in Georgia and a square with fountains, a restaurant and a place for walking. People who come to exhibitions will also find it relevant to rent apartments in this building
Nearby are Kikvidze Park, Mushtaidi Park and Dynamo Arena
Near the Didube metro station, with the market of the same name and a bus station in all directions, including foreign ones, also near the Tsereteli station, the second line of the Tbilisi metro

Privacy Policy

Effective from 10 May 2024.

One of our top priorities is the privacy of visitors to our website, available at https://asgard.llc.

We understand that your privacy is important to you.

This privacy policy informs you of what information we collect about you when you use our website. By collecting this information, we are acting as a data controller and we are required by law to provide you with information about us (referred to as "the Company", "we" or "us" throughout this policy), why and how we use your data, and the rights you have in relation to the processing of your data.

I. What information we collect on the website

Our company may collect personal data in the course of its business activities, including in connection with the provision of certain services.

The following information about you may be collected on https://asgard.llc (the "Site"):

  • (a) data you provide on the site about yourself or in the course of using the site, such as your name, postal address, email address, telephone number, other contact information;
  • (b) data that is automatically transmitted by the site's services when you use them using the software installed on your device, including your IP address, cookie data, information about the ads you see on the site, information about your browser (or other programme used to access the site), the technical characteristics of the hardware and software you use, the date and time you access the site's services and other similar information;
  • (c) the data and information you provide when you contact support.

When you use our site to view the information we provide, we and third parties may use cookies to help the site function, to collect useful information about visitors, and to improve your user experience.

II. Use of information

We only collect and store information that is necessary for you to use the Site or to provide our services to you.

We process information about you for the following purposes:

  • (a) identifying you when you use the website;
  • (b) providing personalised content and services;
  • (c) communicating with you, including sending notices, handling your enquiries and requests;
  • (d) honouring your requests for information;
  • (e) sending out newsletters and alerts;
  • (f) improving the quality of services provided on the site, improving the usability of the site, developing new services;
  • (g) to carry out statistical and other studies.
  • (h) entering into agreements with the Company or others whose goods and/or services may be featured on the site.

We also inform you that the information you provide may be used to send you promotional messages.

III. Your rights

By law, you have the right to request access to the personal data we have collected about you in order to review, amend or delete it.

You can also request that we stop processing your personal data.

You have the right to ask us to stop using your information for a certain period of time if you believe we are doing so inappropriately.

If you would like to make a request to access, review, delete or rectify the personal data we have collected about you or to find out how we process your personal data, please contact us at support@asgard.estate.

We will make reasonable attempts to promptly investigate, fulfil or otherwise respond to your requests as required by applicable law. Various laws may prevent us from providing access to your personal information or otherwise fully complying with your request depending on the circumstances and the request, for example, if providing your information may reveal the identity of another person. We reserve the right to refuse your requests if they may be manifestly unfounded and/or excessive or otherwise objectionable or unreasonable under applicable law.

IV. Your right to file a grievance

If you have a complaint about our use of your information, we would prefer that you first write directly to us at support@asgard.estate so that we can deal with your complaint. However, you may also contact or lodge a complaint with the competent data protection authority, both in your country of residence and in the country of incorporation of our company.

V. How to unsubscribe from our newsletter

If you have registered to receive newsletters, promotional emails or alerts and would prefer not to receive future communications from us, please email support@asgard.estate to unsubscribe from all of our communications.

VI. Disclosure of personal data

The Company will not knowingly disclose or share your personal data and will take reasonable steps to prevent unauthorised or inadvertent disclosure of your data to third parties without your consent, except as required by law and except as follows:

We may provide access to your personal data to third-party service providers hired by the company to provide services related to the site (e.g., customer support). We maintain processes to ensure that any processing of personal data by third-party service providers complies with this Privacy Policy and ensures the confidentiality, availability and integrity of your personal data.

In addition, we may disclose information about you:

  • (a) if required by law or judicial proceedings;
  • (b) to law enforcement agencies or other public officials;
  • (c) where disclosure is necessary to protect the vital interests of an individual;
  • (d) to protect our property, services and legal rights.
VII. Legal basis for processing your personal data

Our processing of your personal data for the purposes set out in section II of this document is based on:

  • (a) on our legitimate interests in promoting and protecting the company, building and maintaining relationships and providing our services;
  • (b) on compliance with the law where certain information is necessary to fulfil our legal or regulatory obligations.

Personal data is processed in accordance with the personal data protection legislation in force in England and Wales, in particular GDPR UK and the Data Protection Act 2018 (DPA).

VIII. Safety

The Company applies generally accepted standards of technological and operational security to protect personal data from loss, misuse, alteration or destruction. However, despite these precautions, the company cannot guarantee that third parties will not gain access to your personal data.

If We leak information about You or suspect misappropriation of personal data by third parties, We will notify You within 24 hours and send instructions on Your actions to minimise the risks.

The information you provide is stored for 3 months from the date you provide it, unless you subscribe to our newsletter or use our other services by completing the relevant form on the website.

In case you subscribe to the newsletter or use other services of the website, personal data will be stored for the entire period of the service and for 3 (three) years thereafter.

If you need to shorten the retention period for personal data, you can write to support@asgard.estate.

Such links do not constitute an endorsement, sponsorship or recommendation by us of the third party, the third party site or the information contained therein, and we are not responsible for your use of them. Such use must be in accordance with the terms of use and privacy policy applicable to those sites.

XI. Minors

This site is not directed to anyone under the age of 18, and we do not knowingly collect or store personal information from anyone under that age.

XII. Updates to this Privacy Policy

We regularly review and, where necessary, update this privacy policy as our services and use of personal data evolve. If we want to use your personal data in a way that we have not previously identified, we will contact you to provide information about this and, if necessary, seek your consent.

We will update the date of this document each time a change is made to it.
